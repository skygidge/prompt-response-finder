### 3. Case Study — Thomson Reuters v. Ross Intelligence Third Circuit (Score: 7)
**Category:** ai_policy | **Article date:** June 11, 2026

First federal appellate hearing on AI training fair use happened June 11 with almost no press coverage. The Third Circuit heard Thomson Reuters v. Ross Intelligence — whether Westlaw headnotes are copyrightable, and whether internal AI training is transformative use. District Court ruled against Ross in 2023; this is the appeal. Will be binding precedent for the circuit and a signal for every AI copyright case.

**Headline EN:** Case Study | **ZH:** 先例之争
**Source:** Legal news / World Trademark Review
